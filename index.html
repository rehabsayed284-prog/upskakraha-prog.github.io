AIM

The aim of the Upper Primary School is to provide quality education that promotes intellectual growth, moral values, discipline, and social responsibility, enabling students to become confident, knowledgeable, and responsible citizens.

OBJECTIVE

1. To ensure strong academic foundations in core subjects.

2. To develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

3. To instill moral values, discipline, and ethical behavior.

4. To promote physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

5. To encourage creativity, teamwork, and leadership.

6. To provide exposure to co-curricular and life skills.

7. To prepare students for secondary education and responsible citizenship.
